The Best Movies According to Salim

My top 3 movies of all time

Eiffel (on HBO Max)

Romantic drama that fictionalizes the story of Gustave Eiffel (Romains Duris), the engineer who finds the inspiration to build the tower that bears his name when he meets Adrienne (Emma Mackey), a mysterious woman from his past.

Doom Patrol

Doom Patrol is a superhero team from DC Comics.

Suicide squad

A secret government agency recruits some of the most dangerous incarcerated super-villains to form a defensive task force.
